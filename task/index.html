Delicious Pasta

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta
  • 100g cherry tomatoes
  • 50g parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Cooking Time & Difficulty

Cooking Time 20 minutes
Preparation Time 10 minutes
Difficulty Easy

Instructions

  1. Boil water in a large pot.
  2. Add the pasta and cook according to the package instructions.
  3. In a pan, heat olive oil and saut√© chopped garlic.
  4. Add cherry tomatoes and cook until soft.
  5. Drain the pasta and mix with the tomato mixture.
  6. Serve with grated parmesan, salt, and pepper.
