Delicious PanCake

  • Ingridents
  • Instructions
  • Cooking Time & Difficulty level

Ingridents

  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 tsps baking powder
  • 2 tsps sugar
  • Pinch of salt
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 1 egg (beaten)
  • 2 tbsps canola or vegetable oil
  • 2-3 tbsps butter (as a substitute for oil)

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt..
  2. Whisk egg, milk, and melted butter.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients.
  4. Heat a pan and grease it.
  5. Pour batter and cook until bubbles form.
  6. Flip and cook until golden brown.
