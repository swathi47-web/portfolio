Prawn's Curry Recipe

Ingredients

  • 500g prawns, cleaned and deveined
  • 500g prawns, cleaned and deveined
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Juice of 1/2 lemon
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2-3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 2 medium tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. Marinate the Prawns:Combine cleaned prawns with spices and let sit for 15-20 minutes.
  2. Prepare the Base: Heat oil, add cumin seeds, and sauté chopped onions until golden brown.
  3. Add Aromatics: Stir in garlic, ginger, and green chilies. Sauté until fragrant.
  4. Incorporate Tomatoes: Add pureed tomatoes and cook until oil separates.
  5. Cook the Prawns: Add marinated prawns and cook until pink and opaque.
  6. Add Coconut Milk: Pour in coconut milk and simmer for 5 minutes.
  7. Finish with Spices:Stir in garam masala and adjust seasoning.
  8. Garnish and Serve:Garnish with cilantro and serve hot with rice or bread.

Cooking Time

Cooking Time 30 minutes
Preparation time 25 minutes
Difficulty Level Easy

Chicken Curry Recipe

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 tablespoons curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 bay leaf
  • ½ teaspoon grated fresh ginger root
  • ½ teaspoon white sugar
  • salt to taste
  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cut into bite-size pieces
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • ¾ cup coconut milk
  • ½ lemon, juiced
  • ½ teaspoon cayenne pepper

Instructions

  1. Heat Oil: In a large pan, heat the oil over medium heat.
  2. Sauté Onions: Add the chopped onions and sauté until they turn golden brown.
  3. Add Garlic and Ginger: Stir in the minced garlic and grated ginger, cooking for another 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  4. Add Tomatoes:Add Tomatoes: Add the tomato puree or chopped tomatoes. Cook until the mixture is well combined and the tomatoes break down.
  5. Spices:Mix in the curry powder, turmeric, and chili powder. Cook for 2-3 minutes to release the spices’ flavors.
  6. Add Chicken:Add the chicken pieces, stirring to coat them in the spice mixture. Cook for about 5-7 minutes until the chicken is browned on all sides.
  7. Add Coconut Milk: Pour in the coconut milk (or water) and bring the mixture to a simmer.
  8. Cook:Cover and let it simmer for about 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and tender. Stir occasionally.
  9. Season: Add salt to taste. Adjust the spices if needed.
  10. Garnish: Remove from heat and garnish with fresh cilantro.

Cooking Time

Cooking Time 25 minutes
Preparation time 30 minutes
Difficulty Level Easy

Fish Curry Recipe

Ingredients

  • 500g fish fillets (like salmon, tilapia, or your choice)
  • 2 tablespoons oil (vegetable or coconut)
  • 1 large onion (finely chopped)
  • 2-3 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1-inch piece of ginger (grated)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed or chopped)
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder (or garam masala)
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1 cup coconut milk (or water)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Fresh cilantro (for garnish)
  • Juice of 1 lemon (optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat Oil: In a large pan, heat the oil over medium heat.
  2. Sauté Onions:Add the chopped onions and sauté until they turn golden brown.
  3. Add Garlic and Ginger: Stir in the minced garlic and grated ginger, cooking for another 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  4. Add Tomatoes: Add the tomato puree or chopped tomatoes. Cook until the mixture is well combined and the tomatoes break down
  5. Spices: Mix in the curry powder, turmeric, and chili powder. Cook for 2-3 minutes to release the spices' flavors.
  6. Add Coconut Milk: Pour in coconut milk and simmer for 5 minutes.
  7. Add Fish: Carefully place the fish fillets in the pan. Spoon some sauce over the fish and simmer for about 10-15 minutes, or until the fish is cooked through and flakes easily with a fork.
  8. Season: Add salt to taste and lemon juice if desired.
  9. Garnish: Remove from heat and garnish with fresh cilantro.

Cooking Time

Cooking Time 30 minutes
Preparation time 15 minutes
Difficulty Level Easy

Mutton Curry Recipe

Ingredients

  • 500g mutton (cut into pieces)
  • 3 tablespoons oil (vegetable or mustard)
  • 2 large onions (finely chopped)
  • 4-5 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1-inch piece of ginger (grated)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed or chopped)
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder (or garam masala)
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1 cup yogurt (whisked)
  • 1 cup water (adjust for desired consistency)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Fresh cilantro (for garnish)
  • 2-3 green chilies (slit, optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat Oil: In a large pan, heat the oil over medium heat.
  2. Sauté Onions:Add the chopped onions and sauté until they turn golden brown.
  3. Add Garlic and Ginger: Stir in the minced garlic and grated ginger, cooking for another 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  4. Add Tomatoes: Add the tomato puree or chopped tomatoes. Cook until the mixture is well combined and the tomatoes break down
  5. Spices: Mix in the curry powder, turmeric, and chili powder. Cook for 2-3 minutes to release the spices' flavors.
  6. Add Mutton: Add the mutton pieces and stir to coat them in the spice mixture. Cook for about 5-7 minutes until browned.
  7. Add Yogurt:Lower the heat and add the whisked yogurt, mixing well to combine.
  8. Simmer: If using a pressure cooker, add water and cook for about 15-20 minutes (3-4 whistles) until the mutton is tender. If using a regular pot, add water and simmer for about 1-1.5 hours until tender, stirring occasionally.
  9. Final Adjustments: Adjust the consistency by adding more water if needed. Add slit green chilies for extra heat, if desired.
  10. Garnish:Remove from heat and garnish with fresh cilantro.

Cooking Time 2hr 30min
Preparation time 30minutes
